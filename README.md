# 中国微米纳米技术学会 | Chinese Society of Micro-Nano Technology (CSMNT)

中国微米纳米技术学会（CSMNT）成立于2005年4月5日，挂靠于清华大学，业务主管单位为教育部，登记注册在民政部。学会旨在推动微米纳米技术的科研、产业化和学术交流，是全国从事微米纳米技术领域的科技工作者及相关单位自愿组成的非营利性学术团体。  

The Chinese Society of Micro-Nano Technology (CSMNT), established on April 5, 2005, is affiliated with Tsinghua University, supervised by the Ministry of Education, and registered with the Ministry of Civil Affairs. CSMNT aims to promote research, industrialization, and academic exchange in micro-nano technology. It is a national non-profit academic organization composed of professionals and institutions engaged in this field.

---

## 我们的使命 | Our Mission

- 推动微米纳米技术基础研究与工程应用的发展  
- 组织学术交流、专业展览及国际合作活动  
- 搭建全国微米纳米技术工作者的联系与合作平台  

- Promote the development of fundamental research and engineering applications in micro-nano technology  
- Organize academic exchanges, professional exhibitions, and international cooperation  
- Build a platform for collaboration among micro-nano technology professionals nationwide

---

## 微米纳米技术 | Micro-Nano Technology

微米纳米技术是一个高度交叉的新兴科技领域，结合电子、机械、材料、制造、检测、物理、化学和生物等多个学科。它是21世纪前沿科技的重要组成部分，正迅速发展并对人类社会产生深远影响。  

Micro-nano technology is an emerging, highly interdisciplinary field integrating electronics, mechanics, materials, manufacturing, testing, physics, chemistry, and biology. As a frontier science of the 21st century, it is rapidly evolving and having a profound impact on society.
